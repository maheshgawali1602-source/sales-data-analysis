# Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

*This Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations that help stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Project Objectives
-Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
-Identify top-performing product categories and customer segments.
-Track monthly sales and profit trends.
-Evaluate regional and state-wise business performance.
-Support business decision-making through interactive analytics.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
-Power BI Desktop
-Power Query
-DAX
-Data Modeling
-Data Visualization

📈 Key Insights
1. Sales Analysis
-Technology and Office Supplies categories contributed significantly to overall sales revenue.
-Sales performance varied across different product categories and regions.
2. Profit Analysis
-Certain categories generated high sales but lower profit margins, indicating opportunities for cost optimization.
-Profit trends helped identify the most profitable business periods.
3. Customer Segment Analysis
-Consumer segment contributed the highest share of sales.
-Customer segmentation helped identify valuable customer groups for targeted marketing strategies.
4. Regional Performance
-State-wise and region-wise analysis revealed top-performing markets.
-Underperforming regions were identified for future growth opportunities.
5. Trend Analysis
-Monthly sales and profit trends highlighted seasonal demand patterns.
-Trend analysis can support forecasting and inventory planning.

📊 Key Metrics
-Total Sales
-Total Profit
-Sales by Category
-Sales by Customer Segment
-Sales by Region
-State-wise Sales Distribution
-Monthly Sales Trend
-Monthly Profit Trend

🚀 Business Impact
-Improved visibility into business performance.
-Enabled data-driven decision-making.
-Identified growth opportunities across products, customers, and regions.
-Provided actionable insights through interactive dashboards.
